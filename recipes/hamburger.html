Easy Airfryer Hot Dogs

The Classic Burger but oh so easy
This takes no time and you'll be enjoying a classic american cuisine in the comfort of your own home!
Enjoy!



Ingredients

  • 1 Large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground black ppper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 4 hamburger rolls
  • Toppings and condoments of choice

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.

  2. Step 2

    Whisk together egg, salt, and pepper in a medium bowl. Add ground beef and bread crumbs and mix with your hands or a fork until well blended. Form into four 3/4-inch-thick patties.

  3. Step 3

    Place patties on the preheated grill. Cover and cook 6 to 8 minutes per side, or to desired doneness. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).

  4. Step 4

    Assemble burgers on buns with toppings of your choice
